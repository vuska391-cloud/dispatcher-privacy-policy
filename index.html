Privacy Policy

Dispatcher Board Extension

This Privacy Policy describes how the Dispatcher Board Extension processes data when used by authorized personnel of [Company Legal Name].

1. Purpose of the Extension

The extension is intended for internal operational use only. Its single purpose is to display and support management of dispatcher assignments, employee allocation, office lists, and related scheduling information in real time for authorized company staff.

2. Data We May Process

The extension may process limited data required for its core functionality, including:

  • employee names;
  • work email addresses;
  • roles and assignment information;
  • office and scheduling data;
  • internal operational status data displayed in the board interface.

The extension is not intended to collect medical information, financial or payment data, browsing history, personal communications, or website content unrelated to the internal functionality of the extension.

3. How We Use Data

We use processed data only to:

  • display the internal dispatcher board;
  • show current assignments and employee allocation;
  • support internal scheduling and operational coordination;
  • maintain and improve the core functionality of the extension.

4. Where Data Is Sent

Data may be transmitted only to systems required for the operation of the extension, including authorized internal company services and infrastructure such as:

  • the company Jira environment at servicedesk.azerconnect.az;
  • the database/infrastructure used to store and update operational board data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We do not transfer user data to third parties except where necessary to operate the extension, comply with applicable law, protect rights and security, or work with service providers strictly required for the extension’s internal functionality.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the internal operational function of the extension and in accordance with the company’s internal retention and security policies.

7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data in transit and at rest. Access to the extension and related data is limited to authorized users and systems.

8. Chrome Web Store Compliance

The use of information received by this extension complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
